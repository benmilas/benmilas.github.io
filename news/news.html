Milwaukee Bucks Acquire Jae Crowder

The Milwaukee Bucks have acquired forward Jae Crowder from the Phoenix Suns as part of a four-team trade that also includes the Brooklyn Nets and Indiana Pacers. To complete the deal, the Bucks have traded guard George Hill, center Serge Ibaka, forward Jordan Nwora and three second-round draft picks to the Pacers and two second-round draft picks to the Nets.

While some may question the cost of giving up three players and multiple draft picks for Crowder, the Bucks may see it as a necessary move to strengthen their chances of making a deep playoff run. With Giannis Antetokounmpo leading the way, the team is already considered one of the top contenders in the Eastern Conference, and adding a player like Crowder could make them even more formidable.

Giannis Antetokounmpo and Jrue Holiday named to 2023 All-Star Game

Giannis Antetokounmpo and Jrue Holiday have been selected to play in the 2023 NBA All-Star Game! Giannis finished second in the fan vote, which means he will be a team captain facing off against Team LeBron. The two stars will join the best players in the league in representing the Eastern Conference in the All-Star Game. This marks Giannis' seventh consecutive All-Star selection and Jrue's second selection. Congratulations to both players on this well-deserved honor!
